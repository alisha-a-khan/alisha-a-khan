### About Me 🧕🏽💻
My name is Alisha. I am a leader in my community, an active learner, and I love data analysis!

***Background*** 
: I am pursuing my B.Sc. in Computer Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology and I am expected to graduate in May 2023. My coursework is specialized in Data Science. 

Along with school, I am currently a SoReMo Research Fellow. SoReMo is a network of faculty and professionals that provide a framework for social responsibility to become a core value in research and education at Illinois Tech. I am working jointly with SoReMo faculty on a funded research, writing, and outreach project that involves investigating and developing strategies to address faculty and student diversity at Illinois Tech.

In my free time, I enjoy working on fulfilling projects where I can apply my talents and make change. I was the Co-founder and Vice President of Women and Gender Minorities in STEM (WiSTEM) at the Illinois Institute of Technology for the 2020-2021 Academic Year. I really love graphic design; I have been developing my skills for over 7 years. I applied this to WiSTEM as I handled our marketing strategies. I have also made a website that provides a great experience to users who access it. I love furthering my computer science skills by applying them to WiSTEM. I created a web-scraper to get email addresses of the department heads so I could advertise events to them. In addition, I am very passionate about the cause because there are not a lot of women and gender minorities in my major. I wanted to build a community and that is why I co-founded the organization.

I am furthering my leadership skills by being a Women in Tech Fellow at Accenture, as part of their Student Leadership Program. I was selected as one of thirty women to participate in a program designed to enhance the professional development of underrepresented students. The program teaches me about consulting and how to achieve great client impact. I also completed a Technical Resilience mentoring program at Microsoft.

***Mission Statement***: I care about tech for social good, particularly in diversity, equity, and inclusion. I want to use my skills to solve challenging research, design, and engineering problems that will contribute to society's betterment. 

***My Philosophy***: Grasp every opportunity that comes my way and use it to better myself.

***Interests***: Data Analytics, Statistics, Machine Learning
