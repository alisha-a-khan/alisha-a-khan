### About Me 💟🧕🏽
My name is Alisha. I am a leader in my community, an active learner, and I love software engineering as well as data analysis!

***Background.*** 
I am pursuing my B.Sc. in Computer Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology and I am expected to graduate in May 2023. My coursework is specialized in Artificial Intelligence. 

Along with school, I am currently a SoReMo Research Fellow. SoReMo is a network of faculty and professionals that provide a framework for social responsibility to become a core value in research and education at Illinois Tech. I am working jointly with SoReMo faculty on a funded research, writing, and outreach project that involves investigating and developing strategies to address faculty and student diversity at Illinois Tech.

In my free time, I enjoy working on fulfilling projects where I can apply my talents and make change. I am the Co-founder and Vice President of Women and Gender Minorities in STEM (WiSTEM) at the Illinois Institute of Technology. I really love graphic design; I have been developing my skills for over 7 years. I apply this to WiSTEM as I handle our marketing strategies. I have also made a website that provides a great experience to users who access it. I love furthering my software engineering skills by applying them to WiSTEM. I created a web-scraper to get email addresses of the department heads so I could advertise events to them. In addition, I am very passionate about the cause because there are not a lot of women and gender minorities in my major. I wanted to build a community and that is why I co-founded the organization.

I am furthering my leadership skills by being a Women in Tech Fellow at Accenture, as part of their Student Leadership Program. I was selected as one of thirty women to participate in a program designed to enhance the professional development of underrepresented students. The program teaches me about consulting and how to achieve great client impact.


***Mission Statement.*** I care about tech for social good, particularly in diversity, equity, and inclusion. I want to use my skills to solve challenging research, design, and engineering problems that will contribute to society's betterment. 

***What it's like working with me.*** I put my all into whatever I am involved in. I make sure that the quality of my work matches the high standards I have of myself. 

***My Philosophy.*** Grasp every opportunity that comes my way and use it to better myself.

***Interests.*** Software Engineering, Data Analytics
